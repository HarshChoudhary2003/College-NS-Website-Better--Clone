Courses Offered

Govt. Degree College Nagrota Surian offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to meet the academic and career aspirations of our students. Our curriculum is regularly updated to align with industry standards and the demands of a globalized world.

Eligibility Criteria & Admission Process

Eligibility criteria vary by program. Generally, for undergraduate courses, candidates must have passed 10+2 (or equivalent) from a recognized board. For postgraduate diplomas, a bachelor's degree in any discipline is usually required.

Detailed eligibility criteria and the admission process are available on our Admission Page. We encourage all prospective students to review these details carefully before applying.

Note: Admission is based on merit and availability of seats as per Himachal Pradesh University guidelines.
